ABOUT ME

I am brand new to the world of Web Development, although I do have vast experience in technology systems management. For the last fourteen years I served as the Vice President and General Manager at Main & High Shortstop, which was the convenience store owned by my parents. I, along with my wife, managed twenty-two employees, placed all orders, and managed all inventory. In addition to those duties, I was also solely in charge of both the upkeep and continued functioning of the point of sale computers, as well as the back office software that served them.

My parents recently retired, and my wife and I decided not to continue on with the business, but instead to forge a new path for ourselves. I enrolled in a Coding BootCamp and have been studying hard to learn all of the material. It has been challenging so far, but I look forward to the future and all that I still have to learn.
